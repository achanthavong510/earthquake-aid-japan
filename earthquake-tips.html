Earthquake Safety Tips
地震の安全対策

  • Prepare an Emergency Kit (緊急用持ち出し袋を準備)
  • Secure furniture and heavy items (家具や重い物を固定)
  • Know evacuation routes (避難経路を確認)
  • Discuss a family emergency plan (家族で緊急時の連絡方法を話し合う)
  • Keep shoes and flashlight by your bed (枕元に靴と懐中電灯を用意)
  • Drop, Cover, and Hold On (身を低くし、頭を守り、動かない)
  • Stay away from windows and glass (窓やガラスから離れる)
  • Protect your head and neck (頭と首を守る)
  • If outside, move to an open area (屋外では広い場所へ)
  • If driving, stop safely and stay in the car (運転中は安全な場所に停車し車内で待機)
  • Check for injuries and hazards (けがや危険箇所を確認)
  • Follow official information and updates (公式情報を確認)
  • Be ready for aftershocks (余震に注意)
  • Help neighbors if safe (安全なら近所の人を助ける)
  • Evacuate if necessary (必要に応じて避難)
